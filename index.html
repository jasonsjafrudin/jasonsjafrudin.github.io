ABOUT ME

Data-driven storyteller. I am passionate about transforming data into actionable insights that drive processes and improve outcomes. With expertise spanning data science, data engineering, and data analytics, I possess a distinctive ability to connect the realms of engineering and product understanding for any business. My proficiency includes ETL, data warehousing, data modeling, data visualization, dashboarding, machine learning, and advanced statistical techniques—skills I leverage to drive innovation and advance the frontier of the data revolution.

When I was 19 years old, I completed my bachelor's degree in Econometrics and Quantitative Economics at University of California, Berkeley. Since then, I have been working in the Business Intelligence and Analytics space as a Data Engineer, Data Analyst, and Data Visualization Expert at various big corporations such as T-Mobile, Columbia Sportswear, and Medtronic. I have also worked on numerous data science projects and implemented statistical techniques to build predictive analysis models. If you want to know more about my projects, check out my portfolio.

My goal is to optimize world processes by using data. If you are interested in connecting with me, feel free to contact me.
